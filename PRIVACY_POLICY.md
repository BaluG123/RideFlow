# Privacy Policy for RideFlow

**Last Updated: December 17, 2024**

## Introduction

RideFlow ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our cycling tracking application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- Google User ID

### 2. Location Data
When you track rides, we collect:
- GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- Route paths
- Distance traveled
- Speed data
- Timestamps

### 3. Activity Data
We automatically collect:
- Ride statistics (distance, duration, calories)
- App usage patterns
- Device information (for analytics)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- **Provide core functionality**: Track and save your cycling rides
- **Sync across devices**: Store your data in the cloud
- **Show analytics**: Display your progress and statistics
- **Send notifications**: Remind you to ride and celebrate achievements
- **Improve the app**: Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your data is stored on your device using SQLite
- **Cloud Storage**: When you sign in, data is synced to Firebase Firestore
- **Location**: Data is stored in Firebase servers (location: [your-region])
- **Security**: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Share your location with advertisers
- ❌ Use your data for marketing without consent
- ❌ Share your rides publicly without permission

We MAY share data:
- ✅ With Google (for authentication only)
- ✅ With Firebase (for cloud storage - Google-owned)
- ✅ If required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data in the app
- **Export**: Download your data (contact us)
- **Delete**: Remove your account and all data
- **Opt-out**: Disable cloud sync and use locally only

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data stored indefinitely while you use the app
- **Deleted accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Inactive accounts**: Data retained for 2 years, then deleted

## Children's Privacy

RideFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app with the updated date.
